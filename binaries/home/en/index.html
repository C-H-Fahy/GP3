HELP


Before opening "Administration" or "local Web", verify that easyphp.exe is running (eg: Start>Programs>EasyPHP>EasyPHP) and that the servers' status is OK (double click on the EasyPHP icon beside the clock [1]: the status of Apache and MySQL must be green).

Administration

Right-click on the EasyPHP icon [1] and select "Administration"[2]. The page displayed allows you to manage your databases and administer Aliases.

Local web

Right-click on the EasyPHP [1] icon and select "local Web"[2] to display your files (see "Introduction to EasyPHP").
